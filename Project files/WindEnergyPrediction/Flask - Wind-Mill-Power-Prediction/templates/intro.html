Wind Turbine Energy Prediction Based on Weather Conditions

Renewable energy, such as wind and solar energy, plays an increasing role in the supply of energy worldwide. This trend will continue because global energy demand is increasing and the use of nuclear power and traditional energy such as coal and oil is unsafe and leads to large amounts of CO2 emissions.
